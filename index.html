John Sun

John is an Interaction Design student at the California College of the Arts. He was born and raised in San Francisco, CA. After studying finance and working professionally for a few years, he decided to pursue a career in design.

What sparked John's interest in design was his desire to solve problems, question the world and its existing designs, and to understand human behavior.

When not working or attending school, John enjoys watching basketball, spending time with his wife and dog, watching movies, and painting.
